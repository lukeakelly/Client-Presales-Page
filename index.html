Your Journey to AI-First, Mapped Out

We guide you through a phased, transparent, and outcome-focused journey, starting with tangible results in weeks, not years. This roadmap ensures we turn vision into reality, step by step.

Phase 1: Immediate & Align (0-3 Months)

This initial phase focuses on internal alignment and setting the foundational blocks. We communicate the AI-first vision, establish governance with a cross-functional AI Strategy Taskforce, and select 2-3 high-impact pilot projects. Sales outreach begins to seed conversations, and we engage actively with Microsoft and Boomi to leverage partner programs. The aim is to build internal excitement and identify first opportunities.

Phase 2: Build & Launch (3-6 Months)

In this phase, we move to execution and initial market presence. We finalise and launch AI offerings like "AI Readiness Assessment" and "AI Pilot Accelerator". Initial pilot projects (internal and external) are completed, with results meticulously monitored to create compelling case studies. A significant skill development sprint ensures our teams gain key AI certifications, and project delivery frameworks are updated to incorporate AI-specific considerations. Marketing efforts begin to establish Atturra's thought leadership.

Phase 3: Scale & Institutionalise (6-12 Months)

Building on early successes, this phase focuses on broadening client engagements and scaling our capabilities. We organise AI Innovation Days, refine existing offerings based on market feedback, and establish a dedicated AI practice within Atturra. Internal systems and tools are upgraded to support broader AI development, and key performance indicators (KPIs) are defined to systematically track our AI transformation, ensuring regular reporting to leadership.

Phase 4: Evolve & Lead (12-24+ Months)

The long-term vision focuses on cementing Atturra's leadership in the AI space. We evaluate opportunities for new business models, such as managed AI services or subscription-based products. Continuous innovation is paramount, with constant monitoring of emerging AI trends. We maintain a balance between AI-led projects and our traditional integration engine. Successful larger AI projects are actively publicised through press releases and industry awards, solidifying Atturra's position as the leading AI Integrator in Australia.
